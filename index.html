"JAPAN PREMIUM FOOD 2025"

Campaign In Collaboration with Hotel Chains in Thailand
– Experience the Authentic Taste of Japan –

The Japan External Trade Organization (JETRO) is hosting this campaign together with leading hotel chains in Thailand.

The Campaign Mission

Japanese food is becoming and more popular across Thailand, with many new Japanese restaurants opening even outside of Bangkok. This shows that Thai people really love Japanese cuisine.

The Japanese government, through JETRO, launched the "JAPAN PREMIUM FOOD" project to collaborate with hotel chain restaurants (in Bangkok and other provinces). Our main goal is to offer Thai consumers and tourists the chance to taste authentic imported Japanese ingredients at these high-quality restaurants across the country.

This project focuses on showing the high quality and appealing taste of these ingredients, sharing authentic Japanese eating styles, and supporting participating restaurants by increasing awareness and sales. Ultimately, we aim to encourage the consumption of Japanese ingredients in various areas of Thailand to ensure sustainable export growth.

Thai People Love
Japanese Food

This love for Japanese food is growing deeper. Many Thai tourists travel to Japan, which helps them learn more about and experience real Japanese food. This has led to three main trends:

  • Seeking Real Quality: People want to eat authentic Japanese food and find out about the best, true Japanese ingredients.
  • New Food Styles: People are also looking for new ideas and trends in Japanese cooking.
  • Good for All Food Styles: We want to show that high-quality Japanese ingredients can be used in many different types of food, not just Japanese dishes.
